“An apology? What are they sorry for?”

“For writing the book. They always are.”

They don’t get very far with the book when there’s a knock at the door. Claude jumps out of her seat. She looks like a woman in a nightmare. “After all they must have followed me,” she says quietly. “We have to hide.” The girl takes her wrist and brings her to the larder and they begin to move the sacks to make a hiding place for themselves, but Claude says “What if they find the steer?”, so go to the master bedroom where the steer is sleeping softly. All the while there has been knocking on the door.

“No one keeps a cow in their house, Claude. If they see it they’ll be suspicious no matter what’s happened.”

“We’ll go out the back and hide over the hill.”

Claude opens the back door.

“Ah, hello. You mustn’t have heard me round the front.”

“Sorry, sir. We have been setting the house right.”

“You know, I said it to myself, I said ‘no one has been here for years. Why look here?’ But something just made me think to come here. And after all I find you’re here to restore it.”

“Yes, sir.”

“So I was right to come. Looks like you really have your work cut out for you!”

“What’s the matter, sir?”

The man wears robes which are faded dull colours by the sun and silvery samite thrown over the shoulders. Claude recognizes him as a priest of Lathander. He explains that there has been a string of robberies and a murder in his diocese and all together that he’s here to arrest Claude if only he knew it. He goes on to talk about the deceased and his family and matters of the parish and many other things that she wishes not to hear. “Sorry, sir, that we couldn’t be more help,” she says, and closes the door.

She comes and puts her head on the steer’s rump and lets out a long sigh. The steer twists around and licks her face and this time she doesn’t push him away. “He didn’t say anything about you,” she says, scratching his ear. “It isn’t so strange, despite what one may have said.” The girl snorts, but she lies down in the hay with Claude and the animal.
